ALL ABOUT SYD

Where am I from?

    I have lived in 6 states, including: South Dakota, Iowa, Kansas, Minnesota, Michigan, and Georgia. My family and I have had to move a lot because of my dad's job. There are pros and cons to moving, and it was hard leaving my friends all the time; however, I am glad I ended up at the University of Georgia for college after moving to Georgia after my senior year.
    Upon moving to Georgia after my senior year, I first took a gap year. During my gap year, I traveled to Italy, Malawi, and Haiti. I also worked as a cashier at Publix after all my traveling came to an end. Oh, and I went skydiving too! (Below is a video from my Malawi trip!)
    Italy was full of adventure and I got to see so many things I had only heard about at one point. It was especially amazing seeing the city of Florence at night, it was so beautiful! In Malawi, I worked at CURE hospital with the kids that were being treated there. My team and I built a playroom for them throughout the week, and got to keep the kids company while they healed from their surgeries. In Haiti, my team and I did prayer walks around the village of Kalapa, led a Vacation Bible School, and got to learn so much about their culture-- we even cooked several meals with them!
    My gap year fostered my adventurous spirit and allowed to go on so many amazing trips and adventures, and that has carried over into my life now. I love all that Athens offers and exploring this amazing city is my favorite thing!
